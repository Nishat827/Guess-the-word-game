GUESS THE WORD

Game Rule

  • You have to guess a word related to family members, which will be hidden. Enter a letter into the input box to complete the guessed word, and if the entered word matches, it will display on the screen.
  • For example, the hidden word could be "son", "daughter", "brother", "father", or "mother".
  • You will get a maximum of five chances to complete the hidden word.
